# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

### Key Interactions
- Works with UX Designer on implementation of designs and usability
- Partners with QA/Testing on acceptance criteria validation
- Collaborates with Business Analyst on requirement clarification

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

### Key Interactions
- Works closely with UX Designer to ensure user needs are met
- Partners with Business Analyst to translate business requirements
- Collaborates with Support Engineer to incorporate customer feedback

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Key Interactions
- Works with Scrum Master to remove impediments and maintain team velocity
- Coordinates with all roles to track progress and escalate risks
- Partners with Product Manager on prioritization and scope management

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers own the user experience and ensure that solutions are usable, accessible, and delightful. They collaborate with Product Managers and Developers to translate user needs into intuitive, well-designed interfaces.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and gather requirements
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Collaborate with Developers to ensure designs are implementable
- Validate designs through user testing and feedback
- Maintain design systems and component libraries
- Champion accessibility and usability best practices

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered, accessible solutions
- Reduce friction in user workflows
- Enable measurable improvements in user satisfaction and engagement

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions with Developers
- User research findings and personas
- Design specifications and component documentation
- Accessibility compliance reports

### Key Interactions
- Works closely with Product Manager to understand user needs and business goals
- Partners with Developers to ensure feasibility and quality implementation
- Collaborates with Business Analyst to refine acceptance criteria from a UX perspective

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business needs and technical solutions. They translate requirements, refine acceptance criteria, and ensure that solutions align with business objectives.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements
- Translate business needs into clear, technical specifications
- Refine acceptance criteria with Product Manager and Developers
- Identify and document dependencies and integration points
- Validate that solutions meet business requirements
- Create and maintain requirement traceability

### Goals
- Ensure alignment between business goals and technical delivery
- Reduce rework and scope creep through clear requirements
- Improve hand-offs between business stakeholders and delivery teams

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documentation and specifications
- Acceptance criteria refinement sessions
- Stakeholder alignment meetings
- Integration point mapping and dependency diagrams

### Key Interactions
- Works with Product Manager to translate vision into actionable requirements
- Partners with Developers to ensure requirements are implementable
- Collaborates with UX Designer to incorporate user experience considerations
- Supports Support Engineer in understanding business workflows

---

## Support Engineer

### Role Summary
Support Engineers are the voice of the customer, capturing production issues, customer insights, and pain points. They close the feedback loop between end-users and the product/development team, ensuring that customer needs drive continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Triage and prioritize customer-reported issues
- Collect and document customer feedback and feature requests
- Communicate customer pain points to Product and Development teams
- Validate bug fixes and feature implementations in production
- Create documentation and troubleshooting guides for customers
- Participate in post-incident reviews and escalation processes

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and reduce support burden
- Provide data-driven insights to inform product decisions
- Close the loop between customers and product development
- Enable fast resolution of critical production issues

### Typical Communication
- Customer issue reports and escalations
- Feature request summaries and customer feedback synthesis
- Support documentation and runbooks
- Incident reports and post-mortem participation

### Key Interactions
- Partners with Product Manager to surface customer insights and trends
- Works with Developers to validate fixes and troubleshoot issues
- Collaborates with Business Analyst to understand business workflows from a customer perspective
- Supports Project Manager with critical issue escalations

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters coach the team on agile practices and ceremonies. They remove impediments, facilitate communication, and help the team maintain a sustainable pace and consistent delivery rhythm.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Coach team members on agile principles and practices
- Identify and help remove blockers and impediments
- Track team metrics (velocity, burndown) and facilitate continuous improvement
- Foster psychological safety and open communication
- Protect team focus and minimize context switching

### Goals
- Enable consistent, predictable delivery
- Improve team collaboration and communication
- Drive continuous process improvement and team health
- Remove obstacles to productivity

### Typical Communication
- Sprint ceremonies facilitation and notes
- Impediment tracking and escalation
- Team metrics and retrospective action items
- Coaching conversations with team members

### Key Interactions
- Works closely with Project Manager to coordinate delivery and escalate risks
- Supports all team roles in removing blockers and maintaining focus
- Facilitates collaboration between Product Manager, Developers, and other stakeholders
- Champions team health and psychological safety
